thai chicken coconut curry

ingredients

  • 2-3 TBSP coconut oil
  • 1 X sweet vidalia or yellow onion, diced small
  • 1 LB chicken breast, diced into bite-sized pieces
  • 3 CLOVES garlic, finely minced
  • 2-3 TSP ginger, ground
  • 2 TSP coriander, ground
  • 13.5 OZ coconut milk
  • 1-1.5 CUP carrots, shredded
  • 1-3 TBSP thai red curry paste
  • 1 TSP salt
  • 0.5 TSP black pepper
  • 3 CUP fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 TBSP lime juice
  • 1-2 TBSP brown sugar
  • 0.25 CUP fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1 CUP rice
  1. add OIL, ONION to skillet
  2. sautee until ONION begins to soften (~5min)
  3. add CHICKEN
  4. cook for 5min or until done
  5. add GARLIC, GINGER, CORIANDER
  6. cook for 1 min, stirring frequently
  7. add COCONUT MILK, CARROTS, CURRY PASTE, SALT, PEPPER
  8. reduce heat to medium
  9. gently boil for ~5min, or until desired volume & thickness
  10. add SPINACH, LIME JUICE, BROWN SUGAR
  11. cook until SPINACH has wilted, abt 1-2 min
  12. sprinkle with cilanto, serve on rice
