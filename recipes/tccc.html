day laborer bibimslop

ingredients

  • 2-3 TBSP coconut oil
  • 1 X sweet vidalia or yellow onion, diced small
  • 1 LB chicken breast
  • 3 CLOVES garlic, ground
  • 2-3 TSP ginger, ground
  • 2 TSP coriander, ground
  • 13.5 OZ coconut milk
  • 1-1.5 CUP carrots, shredded
  • 1-3 TBSP thai red curry paste
  • 1 TSP salt
  • 0.5 TSP black pepper
  • 3 CUP fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 TBSP lime juice
  • 1-2 TBSP brown sugar
  • 0.25 CUP fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  1. add oil, onion to skillet \& sautee
  2. add chicken and cook for 5min or until done
  3. add GARLIC, GINGER, CORIANDER
  4. cook for 1 min, stirring frequently
  5. add COCONUT MILK, CARROTS, CURRY PASTE, SALT, PEPPER
  6. stir to combine
  7. reduce heat to medium and gently boil for ~5 min, or until desired volume \& thickness
  8. add SPINACH, LIME JUICE, BROWN SUGAR
  9. cook until SPINACH has wilted, abt 1-2 min
  10. sprinkle with cilanto, serve on rice
